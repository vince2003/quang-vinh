Biography

I'm a postdoc at Multimedia and Image Processing Lab in the Department of Artificial Intelligence Convergence, Chonnam National University. I earned my Ph.D. from the same university and previously lectured at Eastern International University.

Research Interest

I'm involved in various computer vision challenges, with my current research emphasis being on the following areas:

  • Generative AI

  • Image Restoration

  • Text Image
